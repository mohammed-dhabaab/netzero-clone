Our story

Once, in a patch of barren land, the young Mohammed Al-Khalid (our founder) and his father planted a tiny sapling, an emblem of hope in the midst of emptiness. Years rolled by, and that symbol of hope transformed into a grand tree, standing defiant against the desert's blistering sun. It whispered a timeless truth: even the smallest gestures, nurtured with love and patience, can grow to leave profound marks on the world. Moved deeply by this revelation, Al-Khalid, alongside Dr. Mohammed Alshaikh, embarked on the journey to form Netzero.

With the power of cutting-edge AI technology and the heartbeat of community engagement, Netzero is not just about planting trees. It's about sowing dreams, nurturing spirits, and cultivating a legacy. Our mission is to remind everyone that, like that lone tree, every act of care and love can ripple out, creating vast canopies of change that shelter and inspire generations.

Sustainable Development Goals

photo

Climate action

Our afforestation projects sequester carbon, making the Middle East greener while reducing the need for energy-intensive air conditioning by lowering the overall temperature.

Greener cities

Netzero's 4IR technology digitizes tree planting and maintenance, creating a smart tree database to make afforestation accessible and affordable.

Life on land

The trees we plant create natural habitats for local wildlife, increase land and property value, and improve quality of life by providing shade for people.

Health & well-being

Netzero plants trees to improve air quality and reduce UV exposure and incentivizes outdoor exercise by planting trees to reward participants in walking challenges when they meet their goals.
